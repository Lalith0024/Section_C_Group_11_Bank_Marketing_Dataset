# Lalithendra Kasula - Data Engineering & Analytics Portfolio

Live Portfolio: [https://lalith0024.github.io/portfolio-data-analyst-engineer/](https://lalith0024.github.io/portfolio-data-analyst-engineer/)

This is my professional Data Engineering & Analytics portfolio, custom-built as a high-performance static website using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It serves as a central hub to present my case studies, showcasing my complete data lifecycle capabilities—from building robust ETL pipelines and cleaning unstructured datasets, to engineering interactive business intelligence dashboards.

## Portfolio Projects

### 1. Hotel Booking & Cancellation Intelligence

**Repository:** [SectionC_Group16_Hotel_Analysis](https://github.com/Lalith0024/SectionC_Group16_Hotel_Analysis)

A dynamic, multi-year Tableau dashboard analyzing hotel revenue, booking patterns, and cancellation risk drivers. This project provides deep-dive insights into market segments, lead-time impacts, and seasonal trends to optimize booking strategies.

**The case study covers:**
- Business problem framing & stakeholder context
- Data cleaning & preprocessing workflows
- Trend analysis & lead-time impact
- Interactive Tableau dashboard design
- Strategic insights for revenue optimization

### 2. Global Sales & Profitability Analysis

**Repository:** [Section_C_Group_11_Bank_Marketing_Dataset](https://github.com/Lalith0024/Section_C_Group_11_Bank_Marketing_Dataset)

An interactive BI dashboard delivering geographical and segmental analysis of sales performance. It uncovers loss-making products and dynamically models the financial impact of discounting strategies across regions.

**The case study covers:**
- Geospatial mapping of profitability
- Financial metrics modeling
- Identification of loss-making segments
- Discount strategy impact analysis
- Tableau visualization

### 3. Demographic Customer Profiling

A highly granular analytical dashboard designed to segment customer bases for targeted campaigns. Visualizes behavioral and demographic overlaps to optimize marketing spend.

**The case study covers:**
- Customer segmentation & profiling
- Behavioral data visualization
- Google Sheets dashboard engineering
- Marketing spend optimization strategies
